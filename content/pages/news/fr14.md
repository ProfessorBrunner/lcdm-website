Summary: Professor Brunner was interviewed for the 2014 Faculty retreat.

The University of Illinois interviewed Professor Robert Brunner about
the novel instructional methods being employed in ASTR 330: The Search
for Extraterrstrial Life course. The
[video](http://www.conferences.uiuc.edu/facultyretreat/videos.html) was
aired to the entire faculty at the retreat, and covers the use of an
iPad, youtube, and twitter in a blended learning model for a large,
lecture course.
